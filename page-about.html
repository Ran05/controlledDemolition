Cutting & Corring

about us

Leaders in specialty demolition services

Controlled Demolition is a service contractor excelling in specialty demolition. Our focus is on projects that are technically challenging and in environmentally sensitive areas. Our area of expertise allows us to provide clients with our capacity to undertake time sensitive projects while addressing the needs for sequencing, engineering, craning and hoisting, and shoring of structural elements. We cultivate innovation… We are at the forefront of new techniques and methods to minimize the overall cost of our activities and provide practical approaches to ensure safe, efficient and effective results. Controlled Demolition actively encourages and supports new initiatives that contribute to innovation, staff development, safety, and environmental best practices. Our goal is to always provide clarity and professionalism to a complex construction environment.

Environmental Responsibility

At Controlled Demolition, we are committed to being guardians of our environment. We strive to maximize the recycling potential on all project sites and aim to recycle in excess of 90% of demolition debris. Through our internal programs, we carefully consider the materials to be removed and focus on maximizing the recycling and reuse of materials. Controlled Demolition is committed to providing sustainable services by:

⦁ Building a genuine culture of caring throughout the company and beyond
⦁ Having a positive social and economic impact at a local level
⦁ Putting the health and safety of our workers and clients first
⦁ Constantly seeking to improve the environmental impact of our work

We have worked on a number of LEED (Leadership in Energy and Environmental Design) projects. Based on our extensive experience, we can quickly prepare and completely implement a waste diversion plan.

Our goal has always been to work safely and efficiently while providing our clients the level of service that they deserve.

Innovative Solutions

Technology in demolition has come a long way and at Controlled Demolition we take advantage of these innovations by:
• Applying the use robotics for eliminating the use high risk manual labor
• Investing in emission free equipment
• Using leading edge tools, equipment and protective gear designed for demolition
• Conceptualizing permutations with new and existing technologies to provide our customers with turnkey solutions

Safety

At Controlled Demolition, we understand and follow the text-book approach to managing safety while always considering the need to follow a project schedule and staying on budget. Situations where the interest of schedules, budgets and safety start to diverge are rarely resolved with a one-size-fits-all solution. Controlled Demolition Group excels at safety problem solving; a skill gained from the years of experience in the demolition and construction sectors. Affected workers are consulted and included in discussions concerning safety. We recognize the importance of top-down safety leadership and know that bottom-up contributions often result in safer and more practical solutions to many safety problems.

Community

Controlled Demolition is comprised men and women of varied background from our diverse community. We have created a strong team where no challenge is too great and each project brings us together in a collaborative spirit helping us work with our clients. We believe in the strength of community which has provided our team with a host of benefits. In gratitude, we make our contribution back to the community with our mentorship, sponsorship, and support programs.
